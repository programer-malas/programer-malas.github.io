The Makassar International Eight Festival & Forum, commonly known as F8 Makassar, is one of the largest annual art and culture festivals in Indonesia. It has become a signature event for Makassar, even earning a spot in the Top 10 of Indonesia’s Kharisma Event Nusantara (KEN). The name “F8” represents the eight pillars of creativity showcased during the event:

  • Fashion: Runway shows featuring local and international designers.
  • Food: A massive culinary bazaar with authentic Makassar specialties.
  • Fiction Writers & Font: Literary talks, book launches, and calligraphy.
  • Fine Art: Art exhibitions, installations, and live painting.
  • Folks: Traditional dances and cultural performances from across the archipelago.
  • Flora & Fauna: Exhibitions showcasing exotic plants and animals.
  • Fusion Music: Live concerts featuring national and local artists.
  • Film: Screenings of short films and premieres of local productions.

Hospital Management System (SIMRS)

A comprehensive hospital management system for managing patient information, medical records, billing, and other hospital operations.

Screenshot

SIMRS (Sistem Informasi Manajemen Rumah Sakit) is an integrated information technology system designed to manage the entire clinical and administrative workflow of a hospital. It acts as the “digital nervous system” of a healthcare facility, ensuring that data flows seamlessly between different departments.

  • Inpatient Care: Manages the entire patient journey from admission to discharge, including bed allocation, nursing notes, daily vitals monitoring, and integrated doctor’s orders.
  • Medical Records (EMR): Digital storage of patient history, diagnoses, and treatments for instant access by healthcare providers.
  • Front Office: Handles patient registration, scheduling, and queue management to streamline the arrival process.
  • Pharmacy & Inventory: Tracks medicine stock in real-time, manages prescriptions, and automates procurement alerts.
  • Billing & Finance: Integrates service costs, insurance claims (such as BPJS), and hospital payroll.
  • Laboratory & Radiology: Connects diagnostic results and imaging directly to the patient’s digital file for immediate review.

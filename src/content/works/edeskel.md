Edeskel (edeskel.id) is a comprehensive digital platform designed to modernize village and sub-district administration in Indonesia by transforming traditional workflows into a streamlined, paperless ecosystem. The system integrates essential functions such as population data management, automated public service letter generation, digital attendance tracking for staff, and budget transparency portals into a single, user-friendly dashboard. By centralizing administrative records and providing tools for real-time reporting and community engagement, Edeskel empowers local governments to improve public service efficiency, enhance data accuracy, and accelerate the transition toward becoming "smart villages."

## Key Functionalities

Edeskel provides a suite of tools intended to improve efficiency, transparency, and public service delivery:

### Population & Administrative Data
- Centralized databases for population records (Family Cards, demographic changes)
- General administrative archives — official letters, decrees, and village inventories

### Public Services
- Online letter management system with customizable templates
- Faster and more accessible services for citizens

### Village Resources & HR Management
- Village staff data management
- Digital attendance tracking (photo + location verification)
- Daily activity reporting

### Village Data Bank
- Comprehensive overview of demographic, social, economic, and institutional data
- Supports informed decision-making for local governments

### Digital Presence & Transparency
- Village website portal with budget transparency displays
- Activity galleries and news updates to keep the community informed
